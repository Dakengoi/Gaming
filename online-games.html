1. PUBG: Battlegrounds

PUBG: Battlegrounds is a battle royale game published by Krafton and developed by PUBG Studios. The game is built on earlier mods produced by Brendan "PlayerUnknown" Greene for other games and evolved into a standalone game under Greene's creative supervision. In PUBG: BATTLEGROUNDS, up to 100 players parachute into a secluded island for a winner-take-all battle. In an aesthetically and tactically rich battleground, survivors must discover and scavenge their own weapons, vehicles, and supplies while defeating other players. The goal of the game is to outlive your opponents. You begin the game on an abandoned island with no equipment.
